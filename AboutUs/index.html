About us
Cooperatives are well known and have helped many people in Indonesia, which were formed with the same purpose with the principle of kinship and the spirit of cooperation.

But they began to be abandoned and forgotten. Compete with increasingly sophisticated online banking and lending systems. eKopz here to embrace cooperatives in Indonesia with the mission of digitizing and collaboration between cooperatives.

Because we believe cooperatives can help and prosper more people and SMEs in Indonesia. Because generation Z and beyond have the right to know, cooperatives exist and feel the positive impact.

Milestones

This is where our journey begins

H

2019 - Ideation

This student startup started with brainstorming by 3 main founders of eKopz. The research was then carried out on target users from eKopz including several cooperatives to the Bandung City Cooperative Office. The eKopz team also validated by participating in several business competitions for feedback from professional mentors in their fields.

H

2020 - Developing

Minimum Viable Product eKopz found! The team started prototyping and testing several testers from the cooperative management and the general public. Next, the journey of developing a prototype into complete software begins.

H

2021 - Launching

eKopz meets the first cooperative partner, GIAT Cooperative! The application demo was carried out with the administrators to get feedback and adjust feature requirements for our first partner cooperative. We also invited the cooperation discussion meeting with the Bandung Regency Cooperatives and SMEs Service who were interested in our products.

H

2022 - More Users, More Services

This year eKopz plans that there will be 50 new cooperative partners registered to go digital with eKopz. Not only that, the MSME support and cooperative services for students are expected to be immediately felt by more people.

H

2023 - Soon to be Super Cooperative Apps

In 2023 eKopz plans to develop a cooperative digital wallet feature to realize a cooperative experience that can be accessed securely, anytime, and anywhere.

Our Service

1

COOLAB
(Cooperative Collaboration)

An administration and e-commerce service platform as a forum for collaboration between cooperatives and transparency between administrators and members. Supported by mobile app platforms and websites with excellent features such as Myk cooperative, Kopmin, Kopmart, and Kopnews.

2

UMKM Support
(COMING SOON)

Program for supplying raw materials for the production of cooperative business units in collaboration with partners from MSMEs.

3

Cooperative for Student
(COMING SOON)

A cooperative program for students with features that support student business units, savings and loans.
